Test Soil pH at Any Time

Knowing the pH level of your soil is essential to ensure optimal plant growth. pH test results will guide you in deciding whether and how much the soil needs supplements, like fertilizers and soil pH adjusters.

1. Collect Samples: Take soil samples from the test area at the same depth. Take at least two samples per test, mixing them for a homogeneous sample.



2. Weigh Soil: Take a homogeneous sample and weigh out 1 unit of soil (100g recommended).
3. Add Water: Add 2 units (200ml) of distilled water to the soil.
4. Mix and Wait: - Stir the sample for 30 seconds - Let it sit for 15 minutes
5. Filter and Measure: - Filter liquid into a clean cup - Measure pH using an Adwa pH meter, holding the electrode in the sample for at least 1 minute
